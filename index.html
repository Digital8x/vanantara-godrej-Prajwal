Expertise & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Where exactly is Godrej Vanantara located?
Godrej Vanantara is strategically located on Bannerghatta Road, South Bangalore, directly adjacent to the prestigious Greenwood High Campus. The location offers excellent connectivity to the Pink Line Metro and NICE Road.
What is the total land extent and theme of the project?
The project is spread across a massive 36-acre land parcel, meticulously designed with an "Urban Forest" theme featuring over 3000 trees and dedicated sanctuary zones.
What are the configurations and price points available?
We offer Luxe 2 BHK, Premium 3 BHK, and Luxe 4.5 BHK residences. Prices start from ₹1.28 Cr* for 2 BHK and ₹1.73 Cr* for 3 BHK homes. Detailed cost sheets are available on request.
Is the project RERA approved?
Yes, the project is fully compliant and registered with RERA. The RERA Registration Number is PRM/KA/RERA/1251/310/PR/130526/008653.
What are the key amenities provided?
Residents enjoy a 40,000 Sq.Ft. Clubhouse, temperature-controlled infinity pool, forest trails, organic farming zones, and advanced 5-tier security systems.
What is the current construction status?
The project is currently in the advanced stages of development. Foundation works for the initial towers are progressing rapidly according to the RERA timelines.
Are there any special payment plans available?
Yes, we offer flexible payment milestones linked to construction progress. Exclusive bank tie-ups are also available for competitive home loan rates. Contact our sales advisors for the detailed schedule.
How much area is dedicated to the forest sanctuary?
Over 80% of the total land area is dedicated to open spaces and the forest sanctuary, ensuring every home has a panoramic view of the greenery.
Is the project sustainable and eco-friendly?
Absolutely. We have integrated rainwater harvesting, solar lighting for common areas, organic waste management, and sustainable building materials throughout the development.
Who is the architect behind this forest-themed township?
The project is designed by internationally acclaimed architects who specialize in biophilic design, ensuring a seamless integration between luxury living and nature.

Godrej Vanantara is more than just a residence; it is a meticulously crafted 36-acre forest sanctuary designed to offer a lifestyle of profound tranquility and ecological harmony. Our vision is to provide a haven where modern luxury meets the timeless serenity of nature.

Located on the strategic growth corridor of Bannerghatta Road, this development represents the pinnacle of biophilic living in Bangalore, featuring over 3000 indigenous trees and world-class amenities designed for the elite few who seek a life less ordinary.
